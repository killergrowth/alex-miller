Agent-to-Agent Auction Services

You have a client who wants to sell at auction. Maybe they’ve seen it work for a neighbor’s farm, or the property has been sitting too long on a traditional listing, or the estate needs a clean process with a definite end date.

The problem is you’re not a licensed auctioneer. You can’t run the event yourself. But you don’t want to hand the client to someone else and lose the relationship you’ve built. That’s where Alex comes in.

Seller Representation

Alex provides full seller representation for properties that are better suited to a traditional listing. This includes pricing strategy, market exposure, negotiation, and transaction management through closing.

  • Comparative market analysis & pricing strategy
  • MLS listing and professional marketing
  • Offer negotiation and contract management
  • Transaction coordination through closing

Buyer Representation

Looking to purchase a home or land in central Kansas? Alex represents buyers with the same honesty and professionalism he brings to auction clients. He’ll help you find the right property, evaluate fair market value, and navigate the purchase process with confidence.

  • Property search and evaluation
  • Market value analysis
  • Offer strategy and negotiation
  • Guidance through inspections, financing, and closing

Auction vs. Traditional Listing — Which Is Right for You?

Alex’s advantage is that he can advise you on both options without bias — because he offers both. During your consultation, he’ll walk through the factors that influence the decision:

Factor Auction Traditional Listing
Closing timeline Set date Variable (weeks to months)
Pricing method Competitive bidding List price + negotiation
Condition Sold as-is Inspections & repairs typical
Market exposure Targeted campaign Broad MLS exposure
Best for Estates, unique properties, motivated sellers Move-in ready, standard market conditions
